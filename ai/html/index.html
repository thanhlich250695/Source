News Summarization
Description

Text summarization is one of the most difficult and interesting tasks in NLP today. It helps people to acquire important information in long documents without reading their whole contents.

This challenge focuses only on football match summarization which is a specific domain of online news summarization.

For each input new article, you must determine whether or not it is a football match report then produce the summary of the match follow a template if it is possible. Otherwise, the result should be empty. A match summary includes several kinds of information such as participating teams, match results, goals, cards, substitutions.

A training set with labels is provided for your training. A test set contains only articles without any match annotation.

For each article, a participating system will need to output a match result summary if it is possible, otherwise empty. Teams are scored and ranked by F1 measure on the test set.

You are given:

  • ~500 articles in which events and match summary has been annotated.
  • ~200 articles for testing.
Evaluation Metric

F1 measure is used to rank competing submissions.

Traffic Sign Detection
Description

Traffic Sign Detection is an important component in autonomous systems such as drones and self-driving cars. The detection module must be fast and accurate because subsequent decisions are planned and executed based on the outputs of this system. In this challenge, you will build the best traffic sign detection system given a highly practical real-world dataset.

You are given:

  • 5,000 images of traffic signs with labels.
    • 7 popular Vietnamese traffic signs.
    • Each traffic sign has a bounding box and an associated class.
  • 1,000 images for public leaderboard ranking
  • 2,000 private test images.
Evaluation Metric

Results are computed and ranked based on the mean average precision score mAP@[.5:.95]. Competitors are encouraged to refer to [1] and [2] for additional information about the evaluation metric.

Voice Verification
Description

Voice verification is the process of verifying whether two given utterances belong to a single speaker. In this challenge, we want you to build a speaker text-independent verification model for Vietnamese voices.

We provide a dataset including 10000+ utterances belonging to 500+ Vietnamese speakers, extracted from interview videos uploaded to YouTube.

We allow participants to use external resources to enhance their model.

Evaluation Metric

FAR = The percentage of times a matched pair is rejected by the system

FRR = The percentage of times an unmatched pair is accepted by the system

Frequently Asked Questions
Are there any requirements for participation?

Zalo AI Challenge is open to anyone with interest in AI. However, it is recommended that participants have basic programming knowledge.

I joined previous years of Zalo AI Challenge (and may or may not have won a prize). May I join this year?

Yes! We welcome all AI enthusiasts.

Is it possible to change team members after registration?

Once registered, teams may not add or remove members by themselves. To update team member list, contact the Organizers at aichallenge@zalo.me for support.

How do I receive technical support?

You may post your questions on Discussion tab or email them to aichallenge@zalo.me, and our consultants will assist you accordingly.

When do winners receive their cash prize?

Prizes will be transferred within 30 days after winners provide bank account details and proof of ID after the Awards Ceremony.
